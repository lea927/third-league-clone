What is ThirdLeague all about?

It’s a football management game with a Web3 twist. Own and manage clubs with smart NFTs that grow and evolve based on performance.

What are dynamic NFTs, exactly?

Think of them as living assets. As your players improve or your club wins matches, their value and stats update in real-time.

Why should I buy a club NFT?

Club ownership opens up an entire ecosystem— access Youth Academy prospects, train players, and enjoy exclusive events and perks.

How competitive are matches?

Matches are driven by Al simulations with real-time adjustments, stats tracking, and leaderboard placements. Stay sharp, or get left behind.

How do i make money?

Club & Player NFTs: Own assets that grow in value as you play. Marketplace Fees: Revenue through transfers, upgrades, and training investments. Secondary Market Trading: Player value increases with performance —sell when the time is right!
